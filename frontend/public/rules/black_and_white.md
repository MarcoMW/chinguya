# Black & White Rules

**Black & White** is a 1v1 psychological tile-playing game. Each player has 9 tiles numbered **0 through 8**. 
Even numbers (0, 2, 4, 6, 8) are **Black** tiles.
Odd numbers (1, 3, 5, 7) are **White** tiles.

## Gameplay
Over 9 rounds, both players secretly select a tile to play. The player who plays the higher numbered tile wins the round and earns 1 point. If both players play the same number, the round is a **Tie** and neither player scores. 

All tiles are strictly hidden until the entire match resolves or the Match History Ledger is examined. However, the **color** of the played tile (Black or White) is revealed to the opponent.

## Winning
The first player to win 5 rounds, or the player with the most points after 9 rounds, wins the game. In the event of a total score draw, tiebreakers correspond to who scored their points earliest.
