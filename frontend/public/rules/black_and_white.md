# Black & White Rules

**Source**: Originally played in S2 of The Genius

**Black & White** is a 1v1 tile-playing game. Each player has 9 tiles numbered **0 through 8**. 
Even numbers (0, 2, 4, 6, 8) are **Black** tiles.
Odd numbers (1, 3, 5, 7) are **White** tiles.

## Gameplay
The starting player for the first round is determined by the host.

Over 9 rounds, both players secretly select a tile to play. The player who plays the higher numbered tile wins the round and earns 1 point. If both players play the same number, the round is a **Tie** and neither player scores. The starting player in the next round is the player who scored a point, or if the round was a tie, the player who went second in the previous round goes first. 

All tiles are strictly hidden until the entire match resolves. However, the **color** of the played tile (Black or White) is revealed to the opponent. At the end of the game, the Match History is revealed.

## Winning
The first player to win 5 rounds, or the player with the most points after 9 rounds, wins the game. In the event of a total score draw, tiebreakers correspond to who scored their points earliest.

## Time Controls
Players have 60 seconds for each move. Players also have 5 minutes of reserve time. If a player runs out of time, they lose the game.
